# Exercise 1 — Truth Table

Expression:
(P ∧ Q) → (P ∨ ¬Q)

| P | Q | P ∧ Q | P ∨ ¬Q | Result |
|---|---|-------|--------|--------|
| T | T | T | T | T |
| T | F | F | T | T |
| F | T | F | F | T |
| F | F | F | T | T |

## Explanation
The expression is always true for all values of P and Q, so it is a tautology.
